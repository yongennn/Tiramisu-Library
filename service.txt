Book Borrowing, Members can borrow physical books from a wide range of genres including reference and special collections
Study Rooms, Quiet and group study rooms can be reserved for individual study or collaborative projects
Reference Services, Professional assistance from librarians to help locate resources as well as conduct research and use library tools effectively
Computer and Internet Access, Free access to computers as well as Wi-Fi and printing services for members
Community Programs, Various community engagement initiatives such as reading programs as well as literacy campaigns and cultural events
Access to Special Collections, Members can access unique or historical collections that may require special handling or are not available to the general public
